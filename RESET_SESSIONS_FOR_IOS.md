# GetResett - Complete Reset Sessions Content for iOS App

This document contains all 24 reset sessions organized by emotional state. Each reset is max 2 minutes and uses science-backed techniques.

---

## Emotional States & Color Themes

### 1. Stressed
- **Color Gradient**: Purple (400) to Indigo (500)
- **Description**: Feeling overwhelmed by pressure or demands

### 2. Anxious
- **Color Gradient**: Pink (500) to Rose (600)
- **Description**: Worried thoughts and racing mind

### 3. Restless
- **Color Gradient**: Amber (400) to Orange (500)
- **Description**: Unable to settle or focus your energy

### 4. Overwhelmed
- **Color Gradient**: Indigo (500) to Purple (600)
- **Description**: Too much to handle all at once

### 5. Tired
- **Color Gradient**: Blue (500) to Cyan (600)
- **Description**: Low energy and mental fatigue

### 6. Scattered
- **Color Gradient**: Green (400) to Teal (500)
- **Description**: Difficulty focusing or staying present

---

## Reset Session Structure

Each reset has:
- **ID**: Unique identifier (e.g., `stressed-grounding-walk`)
- **Type**: Either `story` (narrative journey) or `interactive` (game/exercise)
- **Title**: Display name
- **Description**: One-line summary
- **Science Benefit**: Evidence-based benefit
- **Duration**: Total seconds (all are 60-90 seconds)
- **Steps**: Either `storyContent` or `interactiveSteps`

## Implementation Notes for iOS

### Interactive Types Reference:
- **grounding**: No special interaction needed, just display text
- **breathing**: Show breathing cues/animations
- **body-scan**: Progressive relaxation through body parts
- **stress-sweep**: Text input + swipe-away animation
- **bubble-tap**: 8 floating bubbles with tap-to-pop
- **counting**: Counting with breath synchronization
- **rhythm-tap**: Tap following rhythm patterns (left/right)
- **grid-tap**: Grid of circles, tap the highlighted one
- **swipe-sort**: Categorize items by swiping left/right
- **pressure-valve**: Visual pressure bar that decreases
- **dot-connect**: Connect numbered dots in sequence
- **blink-track**: Circle moves in hexagonal pattern around screen

### Color Gradients (Tailwind to iOS):
```
Purple-Indigo: UIColor from #c084fc to #6366f1
Pink-Rose: UIColor from #ec4899 to #e11d48
Amber-Orange: UIColor from #fbbf24 to #f97316
Blue-Cyan: UIColor from #3b82f6 to #0891b2
Green-Teal: UIColor from #4ade80 to #14b8a6
```

### Session Flow:
1. User selects emotional state
2. User chooses 1 of 4 resets
3. Reset plays through steps with timing
4. User rates mood (1-10) after completion
5. If rating < 8: Offer another reset for same emotion
6. If rating >= 8: Return to emotion selector

### Data Storage:
- Track completion count per reset
- Track mood ratings with timestamps
- Track emotional state selection
- Calculate consistency metrics and trends
